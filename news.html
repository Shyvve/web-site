Sports February 22, 2025

Senior Football Team Defends State Championship Title

In a thrilling final played before a packed crowd of 2,400 supporters, Elmswood's senior boys' football team retained the State Championship trophy with a 3–1 victory over Riverside High. Captain Noah Vasquez scored twice, with substitute striker Marcus Osei-Bonsu completing the scoring in injury time. The win makes Elmswood the first school in state history to claim back-to-back state titles in consecutive seasons.

Science March 5, 2025

Year 11 Student Wins Gold at Young Scientists Exhibition

Mei-Lin Zhang of Year 11 has won the Gold Medal at this year's prestigious Young Scientists Exhibition for her groundbreaking project using artificial intelligence to predict early indicators of soil degradation. Her research, developed over eight months with guidance from Dr. Reeves and the school's Innovation Hub, was praised by judges as "genuinely publishable science." Mei-Lin will now represent the school at the European Young Scientists competition in Vienna this May.
