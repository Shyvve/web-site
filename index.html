Our Story

More Than a School — A Legacy

Founded in 1962 by a group of visionary educators, BIL — Bilim Innovation Lyceum has grown from a small community school into one of the region's most respected educational institutions.

Our 28-acre campus, dedicated faculty of over 120 professionals, and a rich alumni network spanning 60 countries are testaments to a tradition built on integrity and excellence.

  • 120+ dedicated faculty members
  • 28-acre modern campus with cutting-edge facilities
  • Global alumni network in 60+ countries
  • Annual scholarship fund supporting 80+ students
